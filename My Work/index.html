About Us

The meaning of food safety

The production and consumption of safe food is a fundamental basis of our society. The success of the food chain depends on the confidence that all parties involved are able to transmit to the final consumer.


Our Cause

We identify the critical points in the food chain and help to elimiate risks by inspections, sampling and chemical and microbiological analysis, allowing appropriate decisions to be made before the product enters the market



Any control that prevents a food safety crisis is priceless.

We make it achievable.



Our Commitment

The guarantee of a service performed on time before the products arrive at the market is the key factor to obtain safe food.
